# Australian Accessibility Organisations

These organisations produce accessibility resources relevant to the project.

---

## People With Disability Australia (PWDA)

https://pwd.org.au

National disability advocacy organisation.

Produces Easy Read resources and advocacy materials.

---

## NDIS Quality and Safeguards Commission

https://www.ndiscommission.gov.au/easy-read-resources

Provides Easy Read explanations of disability rights and services.

---

## Scope Australia

https://www.scopeaust.org.au

Produces accessible communication resources and Easy Read materials.

---

## Australian Government Digital Service – Accessibility

https://www.digital.gov.au/resources/web-accessibility

Guidance on accessibility standards used by Australian government systems.
