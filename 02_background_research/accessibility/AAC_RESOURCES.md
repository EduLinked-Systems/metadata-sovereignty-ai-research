# Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC)

AAC refers to communication methods used by people who have difficulty speaking or writing.

Examples include:

• communication boards  
• symbol systems  
• speech-generating devices  
• AAC apps  

---

## Key Organisations

### ISAAC – International Society for Augmentative and Alternative Communication

https://isaac-online.org

Global organisation promoting AAC research and communication rights.
