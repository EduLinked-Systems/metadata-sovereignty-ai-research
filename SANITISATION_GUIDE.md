# Sanitisation Guide — how to prepare materials for public repo

Use this step-by-step checklist when converting internal materials to public-safe versions.

## Redaction & pseudonymisation steps

1. **Remove PII**: Names, addresses, phone numbers, email addresses, DOB, government IDs. Replace with [PSEUDONYM_1], [LOCATION_X], etc.
2. **Obscure timestamps/IDs**: Replace exact timestamps and case IDs with generic values (e.g., 2026-03-XX, CASEID-XXXX). Remove Drive/SharePoint IDs. (MASTER sheet entries must be private.) :contentReference[oaicite:14]{index=14}
3. **Remove Evidence Details**: Delete evidence registers, chain-of-custody logs, photos, forensic notes — summarise only as “evidence referenced (removed)”.
4. **Remove quotes or testimony**: Do not include direct survivor quotes unless explicit, documented consent for public sharing exists. Replace with a short paraphrase or remove.  
5. **Check for indirect identifiers**: Locations combining age + unique event + profession can be re-identifying — generalise them.  
6. **Confirm consent for media**: For audio/video/images, keep only low-risk illustrative content or synthetic/stock imagery. Never publish raw media of real people without documented consent.
