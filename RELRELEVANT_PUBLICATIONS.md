**Zenodo:** `https://zenodo.org/record/<ZENODO_ID_PIRD>`

**Why:** Methods for preserving authorship and provenance — map this to the METADATA_TEMPLATE and provenance fields students design.

### A Foundational Framework for Ethical Artificial Intelligence
